## Warm-up — 6 min

- 3 min easy bike or jump rope.
- Hip flexor stretch + ankle CARs.
- 5 box jump step-downs (no max effort yet) to prime the system.

## 1. Power — Box Jumps

**Every 90 sec for 5 sets:** 3 reps onto 24" box

**Notes:** Step down between reps (do not rebound). Reset stance, full extension at top, soft landing. Stop if any rep feels sloppy — power work is quality only. Last logged 4/17 at 24" × 10 — keep height, drop reps for crisper output.

## 2. Mixed-Modal Conditioning — Pick ONE

Three options. Pick whichever modality you have access to and *haven't done in 7+ days*. **No KB swings** (already used 4/22 — `§3` #3 rule).

### Option A — Sled push intervals
**5 rounds:**
- Sled push 50 ft heavy (90-135# on the sled)
- Walk back recovery (~60-90 sec)

### Option B — Ski erg intervals
**5 rounds:**
- 30 sec hard, target 90-95% effort
- 90 sec rest

### Option C — Devil press
**4 rounds for time:**
- 6 devil press @ 35# DBs
- 50 ft farmer carry @ 50# each

**Notes:** Each one is a new modality you haven't seen recently. Sled is the top pick (pure conditioning, low CNS hit, fixes `§3` #3). Devil press is the spiciest — use it only if you feel fresh.

## 3. Core Cooldown — 3 sets

- Side plank 30 sec each side
