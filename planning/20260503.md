**Every 90 sec for 5 sets:** 3 reps onto 24" box

**Notes:** Step down between reps, don't rebound. Reset stance, full extension at top, soft landing. Stop early if any rep feels sloppy — power work is quality only. Last logged 4/17 at 24" × 10 — keep height, fewer reps for crisper output.

Pick a modality you haven't done in 7+ days. **No KB swings** (4/22 used the slot). **No row sprints** (Mon 4/27 used the slot).

**Notes:** Sled is the cleanest pick — pure conditioning, low CNS hit, fixes `§3` #3. Ski erg is second. Devil press only if you feel fresh.
