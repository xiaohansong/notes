# Tue 5/5 — Strict Press + RDL (Hinge Floor) + DB OHP

> **Week frame:** Press volume day + the **only loaded hinge session of the week**. RDL is the hinge floor — non-negotiable. DB OHP as hypertrophy push accessory. Anti-rotation core finisher.

**Intensity:** 7/10
**Duration:** ~60 min

## Warm-up — 8 min

- 3 min easy row or jump rope.
- Empty bar press 2×8 → 95# × 5 → 110# × 3.
- Band pull-aparts 2×15.
- Hip hinge with empty bar 2×8 (priming for RDL).

## 1. Strict Press — Strength Volume

**Every 2:30 for 5 sets:** 5 reps @ **120#** (hold) OR **122.5#** (push) — see decision rule

**Decision rule:** if Wed 4/29's session felt RPE 6-7 on set 1, push to 122.5# today. If set 1 felt RPE 7.5+, hold 120#. When in doubt, hold.

**Notes:** Press is parked off 1RM until 5/18 — this is volume-accumulation work. Ribs down, glutes squeezed, drive bar past forehead, lock biceps to ears at top. Brace hard — RDL is later in the session and you want a stable trunk for both lifts. If pushing to 122.5#, set 5 RPE cap is 8.

## 2. DB Overhead Press — Hypertrophy (superset with #3)

**Every 2:30 for 4 sets:** **8-10 reps @ 40#/hand**

**Notes:** Different stimulus than the BB press — full ROM, each side stabilizing on its own. Pick a load where 8 is grinder and 10 is the cap. If you flew through 4/29's at 40#, try 42.5# today.

## 3. Romanian Deadlift — HINGE FLOOR (superset with #2)

**Every 2:30 for 4 sets:** **8 reps @ 210#** (tempo: 2-sec down, controlled up)

**Notes:** **THIS IS THE ONLY LOADED HINGE WORK ALL WEEK. Do not skip.** +5# from 4/17's progression (198#×10 → 195#×10 on 4/23 → 210#×8 today). 8 reps at 210# is the hypertrophy zone, slightly heavier and slightly fewer reps. Bar travels straight down the legs, hinge at hip, soft knees, lats locked tight against the bar. Stop the descent where lat control breaks (mid-shin for most).

If 4×8 @ 210# clears clean ≤ RPE 8, next hinge day try 215# × 8.

**Why this matters more than usual this week:** zero loaded hinge work in last 17 days. Posterior chain is under-fed within an explicit squat-priority phase — hold the floor.

## 4. Anti-rotation Core — 3 sets

- Pallof press 10 reps each side, medium band/cable
- Plus: 30 sec dead bug (alternating)

**Notes:** Pallof was on 4/27's plan but skipped per actual log. Today it actually happens. Quiet hips, resist the cable pulling you to the side. No momentum.

## What to log

Loads/sets/reps under `workout/20260505`. **The watchpoint is RDL** — log the load, the tempo, and any hitching or back rounding. If RDL got skipped or cut short, flag explicitly so it's clear hinge floor was missed.

## Flex rule

If you have to skip Tue, **shift this whole session to Wed** — don't drop RDL. Wed's default is rest, but pressing + RDL there is fine. The press-day pairing is what's portable.
