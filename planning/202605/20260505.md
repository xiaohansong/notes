# Tue 5/5 — Strict Press + RDL (Hinge Floor) + DB OHP

> **Week frame:** Press volume day + the **only loaded hinge session of the week**. RDL is the hinge floor — non-negotiable. DB OHP as hypertrophy push accessory. Anti-rotation core finisher.

## 1. Strict Press — Strength Volume

**Every 2:30 for 5 sets:** `5 reps @ 120#`

**Notes:** holding 120# from 4/29 (5×5 @ 120#, RPE 8 last set). Per the new "only progress when prior session ≤ RPE 8 across all sets" rule, RPE 8 on the last set means hold, not push. Same context as last week — no superset partner added, no rest cut. Press is parked off 1RM until 5/18. Ribs down, glutes squeezed, drive bar past forehead, lock biceps to ears at top. RPE cap 8.

If 5×5 @ 120# clears all sets at RPE ≤ 7 today, *next* press day try 122.5#.

## 2. DB Overhead Press + Romanian Deadlift — superset

**Every 2:30 for 4 rounds:**
- `DB overhead press: 8–10 reps @ 40#/hand`
- `Romanian deadlift: 8 reps @ 200#` (tempo: 2-sec down, controlled up)

**Notes — DB OHP:** different stimulus than the BB press — full ROM, each side stabilizing on its own. Pick a load where 8 is grinder and 10 is the cap. If you flew through 4/29's at 40#, try 42.5# today.

**Notes — RDL:** **THIS IS THE ONLY LOADED HINGE WORK ALL WEEK. Do not skip.** Modest progression from 4/23's 195#×10 (5 sets) → 200#×8 (4 sets). One variable change: shifting to the 8-rep zone with a small +5# bump, dropping a set. Bar travels straight down the legs, hinge at hip, soft knees, lats locked tight against the bar. Stop the descent where lat control breaks (mid-shin for most).

If 4×8 @ 200# clears clean ≤ RPE 8, next hinge day try 205#×8.

**Why RDL matters more than usual this week:** zero loaded hinge work in last 17 days. Posterior chain is under-fed within an explicit squat-priority phase — hold the floor.
