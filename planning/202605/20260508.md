# Fri 5/8 — Squat 3s (Heavy Day) — 345# Top Triple Attempt

> **Week frame:** Heavy squat day. Open at 345×3 with the bail rule preset — no ladder up like 5/1. Hypertrophy backoffs after.

**Intensity:** 9/10
**Duration:** ~70 min — give it space

## Pre-session setup

Decide before warmups: are you opening at **345×3** (PR attempt) or **335×3** (confirm bank)? Default = 345 attempt with bail rule. If sleep was bad, RDL Tue felt grindy, or warmup squats feel off — drop to 335×3 confirm and don't apologize.

Do NOT walk in and let the bar tell you. Pick now.

## Warm-up — 14 min, do NOT rush

- 3 min easy cardio.
- Squat ramp:
  - Empty bar × 8
  - 135# × 5
  - 185# × 3
  - 225# × 2
  - 275# × 1
  - 305# × 1
  - 325# × 1 (last warm-up single — 4-5 min rest into top set)
- Band pull-aparts 2×15 between squat warm-ups.

## 1. Back Squat — Top Heavy Triple

**1 working triple: 3 reps @ 345#** (target RPE 9)

**Bail rules — preset, not in-the-moment:**
- Rep 1 feels RPE 9+ → drop the bar after rep 1 and re-attempt at 335 after 4 min rest. A clean 335×3 today is a productive day; a missed 3rd at 345 is wasted CNS.
- Rep 2 hesitates or grinds → bail. Hesitation = bail rule applies. Don't try rep 3 to "see if it's there."
- Rep 1 and 2 fly → finish rep 3 with full intent. If rep 3 lands clean, that's the PR.

**Notes — read before walking up:**
- Belt and shoes on. 4-6 min rest after the 325# warm-up single.
- 335×3 already banked 5/1. Today is genuinely the next progression — not chasing, not redoing.
- If 345×3 lands clean (RPE ≤ 9), next heavy day target 350# × 3. If it grinds (RPE 9.5), repeat 345 next time. If it bails, repeat 345 with proper rest next time — don't retreat to 335.

## 2. Back Squat — Hypertrophy Back-off

**3 sets, ~3 min rest:** 5 reps @ **295#** (78%)

**Notes:** Lighter than Mon's 315# 5×5 on purpose — you already got the volume work Monday. These are quality-rep back-offs, not a second hard session. Same brace, same depth, smooth tempo. If 295# moves like air across all 3 sets, next heavy day's back-off can go to 300#.

## 3. Single-Leg + Posterior — Optional, skip if cooked

**4 sets, every 2:30:** Bulgarian split squat 8/side @ 30# DB each hand

**Notes:** Hypertrophy single-leg, lighter than Fri 5/1's 35# load — let the back-off squats be the primary leg volume after the heavy triple. Skip entirely if heavy triple was a grinder.
