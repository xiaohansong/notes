# Mon 5/4 — Squat 5s (Volume Day) + Pull-up Retry + Carry

> **Week frame:** Volume squat day, paired with the squat split's lighter rep range. Heavy 3s are Fri 5/8. Carry slot also lives here (Mon–Wed window per programming notes). Pull-up retry at 35#.

## 1. Back Squat — 5×5 Volume

**Every 3:00 for 5 sets:** `5 reps @ 315#`

**Notes:** +10# from 4/27's 305# 5×5 (which was clean RPE 8). 315# = 84% of 1RM, top of the volume zone. Belt and shoes on.

**Decision rule:** if set 1 feels RPE 8.5+, drop to 310# for the remaining sets — don't grind 5 sets at 9. If all 5 sets clear ≤ RPE 8, next volume day try 320#.

This load is also the floor for Fri's hypertrophy back-offs.

## 2. Weighted Pull-up + Face Pull — superset

**Every 2:30 for 4 rounds:**
- `Weighted pull-up: 5 reps @ 35#`
- `Face pull (light cable): 15 reps, slow tempo, 1-sec hold at peak`

**Notes — pull-up:** retry of 4/27's attempt (5/5/5/4 — missed last rep). Today goal: clean 4×5. Full hang to chin-over-bar, no kip. Same load as last attempt — **holding, not progressing**, until 4×5 lands clean. If set 4 fails again, hold 35# for one more session before considering 40#.

**Notes — face pull:** second face-pull session of the week (Sat had 4 sets). Combined = 8 sets, clears the rear-delt deficit. High elbows, pull rope to forehead, hard scap squeeze. **Do not skip — this is the unsexy lift that holds heavy squat under the bar.**

## 3. Suitcase Carry — Carry Slot

**5 rounds, ~60s rest:** `50 ft right hand + 50 ft left hand @ 70–80# DB or KB`

**Notes:** Heavy enough that the last 10 ft is hard. Stand tall, ribs stacked, hand opposite the load doesn't drift. Doubles as anti-lateral core. **Not KB swings** — those are still cycled out.

If carries don't fit time-wise, swap into Sun's flex day (devil press option includes farmer carry). Don't skip both.
