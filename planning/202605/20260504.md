# Mon 5/4 — Squat 5s (Volume Day) + Pull-up Retry + Carry

> **Week frame:** Volume squat day, paired with the squat split's lighter rep range. Heavy 3s are Fri 5/8. Carry slot also lives here (Mon-Wed window per programming notes). Pull-up retry at 35#.

**Intensity:** 8/10
**Duration:** ~70 min — give it space

## Warm-up — 12 min

- 3 min easy cardio.
- Squat ramp:
  - Empty bar × 8
  - 135# × 5
  - 185# × 3
  - 225# × 2
  - 275# × 1
  - 295# × 1 (last warm-up single)
- Band pull-aparts 2×15 between squat warm-ups.

## 1. Back Squat — 5×5 Volume

**Every 3:00 for 5 sets:** **5 reps @ 315#**

**Notes:** +10# from 4/27's 305# 5×5 (which was clean RPE 8). 315# = 84% of 1RM, top of the volume zone. Belt and shoes on. If set 1 feels RPE 8.5+, drop to 310# for the remaining sets — don't grind 5 sets at 9. If all 5 sets clear ≤ RPE 8, next volume day try 320#.

This load is also the floor for Fri's hypertrophy back-offs.

## 2. Weighted Pull-up — Vertical Pull (superset with #3)

**Every 2:30 for 4 sets:** **5 reps @ 35#**

**Notes:** Retry of 4/27's attempt (5/5/5/4 — missed last rep). Today goal: clean 4×5. Full hang to chin-over-bar, no kip. If set 4 fails again, hold 35# for one more session before considering 40#. If 4×5 lands clean, next time bump to 37.5#.

## 3. Face Pull — Rear Delt (superset with #2)

**Pair into the pull-up rest, 4 sets:** 15 reps face pull, light cable, slow tempo, hold at peak 1 sec.

**Notes:** Second face-pull session of the week (Sat had 4 sets). Combined = 8 sets, clears the rear-delt deficit. High elbows, pull rope to forehead, hard scap squeeze. **Do not skip — this is the unsexy lift that holds heavy squat under the bar.**

## 4. Suitcase Carry — Carry Slot

**5 rounds, ~60s rest:** 50 ft right hand + 50 ft left hand @ 70-80# DB or KB

**Notes:** Carries this week's slot per `notes/0426-0501` programming notes. Heavy enough that the last 10 ft is hard. Stand tall, ribs stacked, hand opposite the load doesn't drift. Doubles as anti-lateral core. **NOT KB swings** — those are still cycled out.

If carries don't fit time-wise, swap into Sun's flex day (devil press option includes farmer carry). Don't skip both.
