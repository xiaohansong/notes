# Sat 5/2 — Texas Method Intensity Day (Squat) + Bench Hold

> **Header:** Addresses `Balance §4` (squat: bias loads up; trialing partial Texas Method — Mon volume / Sat intensity), `§3` #5 (bench: hold 185# 5×5 until clean), and `§2` (max-strength testing on a planned ramp day, not impulse).

**Intensity:** 9/10
**Duration:** ~70 min (this is the big day — give it space)

## Warm-up — 12 min, do not rush this

- 3 min easy cardio.
- Squat ramp:
  - Empty bar × 8
  - 135# × 5
  - 185# × 3
  - 225# × 2
  - 275# × 1
  - 295# × 1 (last warm-up rep)
- Band pull-aparts 2×15 between squat warm-ups.

## 1. Back Squat — Texas Method Intensity Single Set

**1 working set: 5 reps @ 320#**

| Set | Load | Reps | Target RPE |
|---|---|---|---|
| Working | **320#** | 5 | 9 |

**Notes — read before lifting:**
- 320# = ~85% of 375# 1RM, and +15# from Mon's 305# 5×5 (which was RPE 8). This is a planned 5RM PR.
- This is **one set**. Do not chase a back-off set, do not "see how 325 feels." TM intensity day is one ugly hard set of 5 and you go home.
- Belt and shoes on. Take 4–6 min rest after the 295# warm-up single before the working set.
- If rep 1 is RPE 8+ already, **drop to 315# or 310#** mid-set decision — don't grind a missed rep at 320 just for ego. A clean 5 at 315 is a productive session; a missed 5th at 320 is a wash.
- If 320 × 5 moves clean (RPE 8 or below), next intensity day target is 325–330#.
- Rest 5 min before moving to bench.

## 2. Bench Press — 5×5 Hold

**Every 3:00 for 5 sets:** 5 reps @ **185#**

**Notes:** `§3` #5 — last bench day (4/26) you missed rep 5 on set 4 at 185#. Repeat 185# until you get all 25 reps clean before moving to 190#. If today's 5 sets all hit 5/5 with the 5th rep not a grinder, *next* bench day move to 190#. If you miss again at 185#, drop to 175# 5×5 next time and rebuild.

Spotter or safety pins for set 4 onwards.

## 3. Horizontal Pull — superset with #4

**Every 2:00 for 4 sets:**
- 70# DB row **10 reps each side**

**Notes:** Pair the rows in between bench sets if you want to keep the session moving — superset bench + DB row alternating works fine. Strong shoulder blade retraction, no momentum.

## 4. Optional Anti-rotation Core (skip if cooked)

- Pallof press 3×10 each side, medium band
