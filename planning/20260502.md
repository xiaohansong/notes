# Sat 5/2 — Heavy Squat Day (Max Focus) + Bench Hypertrophy Hold

> **Header:** Priority lift today is squat — **heavy strength exposure**, format is top-set-and-back-off (NOT a strict TM 1×5). This is the max-focus stimulus per `§4` (squat priority + flexible format). Bench is hypertrophy zone hold per `§3` #5 (185# until clean). Direct rear-delt face pulls again to clear `§3` #2.

**Intensity:** 9/10
**Duration:** ~70 min — give it space

## Warm-up — 12 min, do NOT rush

- 3 min easy cardio.
- Squat ramp:
  - Empty bar × 8
  - 135# × 5
  - 185# × 3
  - 225# × 2
  - 275# × 1
  - 305# × 1
  - 325# × 1 (last warm-up single)
- Band pull-aparts 2×15 between squat warm-ups.

## 1. Back Squat — Top Heavy Triple

**1 working triple: 3 reps @ 335#** (89% of 375# 1RM, target RPE 8.5–9)

**Notes — read before the set:**
- Heavy triples build max strength without the missed-rep risk of a top single. 335# × 3 clean is a strong indicator the 1RM is moving up.
- Belt and shoes on. Take 4–6 min rest after the 325# warm-up single.
- If rep 1 feels RPE 9+ already → **drop to 325# × 3** mid-set decision. A clean 3 at 325 is a productive day; a missed 3rd at 335 is wasted CNS.
- If 335 × 3 is clean (RPE ≤ 8.5), **next heavy day target 345# × 3**. If it's RPE 9 grinder, hold 335# next time.
- Rest 4 min before the back-off sets.

## 2. Back Squat — Hypertrophy Back-off

**3 sets, ~3 min rest:** 5 reps @ **275#** (73% — hypertrophy/groove practice)

**Notes:** Lighter than Mon's 305# 5×5 on purpose — you already got the volume Monday. These are quality-rep back-offs, not a second hard session. Same brace, same depth, smooth tempo. If 275# moves like air across all 3, *next* heavy day's back-off can go to 285#.

## 3. Bench Press — Hypertrophy 5×5 Hold

**Every 3:00 for 5 sets:** 5 reps @ **185#**

**Notes:** `§3` #5 — 4/26 you missed rep 5 on set 4. Today: get all 25 reps clean before earning 190# next bench day. If you miss again at 185#, drop to 175# next time and rebuild. Spotter or safety pins for sets 4–5.

## 4. DB Row — Hypertrophy Horizontal Pull (superset with bench)

**Pair with bench rest:** 4 sets × **10 reps each side @ 70# DB**

**Notes:** Alternate bench → row → rest cycle if you want to keep the session moving. Strong scap retraction, no momentum, full stretch at bottom. Hypertrophy zone (10 reps).

## 5. Face Pull Finisher

**3 sets:** 15 reps face pull, light cable, rest 60s

**Notes:** Second face-pull session of the week. Combined with Wed's 4 sets, this puts you at 7 sets — **above the ≥6/wk target** in `§3` #2. The deficit clears today.

## 6. Optional Anti-rotation Core (skip if cooked)

- Pallof press 3×10 each side, medium band

## What to log

Loads/sets/reps under [workout/20260502](workout/20260502). **For the heavy triple** — log the load, the RPE you actually felt, whether all 3 reps were the same speed, and whether you dropped weight mid-set decision. That's the data that decides 345# vs 335# next heavy day.
