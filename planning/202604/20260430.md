# Thu 4/30 — Hypertrophy Posterior + Carry Conditioning

> **Header:** Pure hypertrophy day per `§4` ("hypertrophy bias on non-priority lifts" — squat/press are the strength priorities, hinge gets the growth stimulus). RDL drops to 8-rep hypertrophy zone, BB row paired for back thickness. Conditioning addresses `§3` #3 (modality variety — sandbag/farmer carry, NO KB swing this week — 4/22 used the slot).

**Intensity:** 6.5/10
**Duration:** ~55 min

## Warm-up — 8 min

- 5 min easy row or bike.
- Hip hinge with empty bar 2×8.
- Glute bridges 2×10.
- RDL ramp: 95# × 8, 135# × 5, 175# × 3.

## 1. Romanian Deadlift — Hypertrophy

**Every 2:30 for 4 sets:** **8 reps @ 205#** (tempo: 2-sec down, controlled up)

**Notes:** 8 reps at 205# is the hypertrophy progression from your 4/17 (198# × 10) and 4/23 (195# × 10) sessions — slightly heavier, slightly fewer reps, same growth zone. Bar travels straight down the legs, hinge at hip, soft knees, lats locked tight against the bar the whole way. Stop the descent where lat control breaks (mid-shin for most). If all 4 × 8 feel clean ≤ RPE 8, next hinge day try 210# × 8.

## 2. Barbell Row — Hypertrophy Horizontal Pull

**Every 2:00 for 4 sets:** **10 reps @ 95#** (tempo 2010 — 2-sec lower, no pause, 1-sec pull)

**Notes:** Match your 4/23 BB row session (95# × 10 × 5). Today drop to 4 sets to leave gas for carries. Torso ~45°, pull to lower sternum, full stretch at bottom. Don't let it become a hip-thrust row — keep the brace.

## 3. Single-leg Hypertrophy

**Every 2:00 for 3 sets:** Walking lunge **20 steps total (10/leg) @ 30# DBs**

**Notes:** `§1` shows you're slightly quad-biased (1.21:1) and heavy on uni already (1.67:1) — that's fine, this isn't pulling more imbalance, it's hypertrophy work for the legs without re-hammering the squat groove the day before Sat's heavy day. Long stride, knee tracks over toe, stand tall.

## 4. Loaded Carry — Conditioning (NO KB SWINGS)

**5 rounds, ~90s rest:**
- Sandbag bear hug carry OR farmer carry: **40 ft down + 40 ft back** at 70-100# total
- Walk back

**Notes:** This is the conditioning piece. **No KB swings** — `notes/0420-0425` cap is 1/wk and 4/22 used it. Stand tall, ribs stacked, breathe through nose. Heavy enough that the last 10 ft is hard. Doubles as anti-lateral core (`§1` core — anti-rotation).

## 5. GHD Back Extension — Anti-flexion Core

**3 sets:** 10 reps, last rep 10-sec hold at top, ~60s rest.

**Notes:** Same as 4/21. Squeeze glutes hard at top, do not hyperextend. The 10-sec hold is the point.
