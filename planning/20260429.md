# Wed 4/29 — Strict Press Volume + Vertical Pull + Rear Delt

> **Header:** Addresses `Balance §4` (press volume-only mode, no 1RM attempts before ~5/18), `§3` #1 (vertical pull closing the deficit — chin-up accessory), and `§3` #2 (rear delt still under target — face pulls finisher).

**Intensity:** 6.5/10
**Duration:** ~50 min

## Warm-up — 8 min

- 3 min easy row or jump rope.
- Empty bar press 2×8, then 95# × 5, 110# × 3 → into working sets.
- Band pull-aparts 2×15 (rear delt prime).

## 1. Strict Press — Volume (Texas-style, sub-max)

**Every 2:30 for 5 sets:** 5 reps @ **120#**

| Set | Load | Reps | RPE target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 120# | 5 | 6 |
| 2 | 120# | 5 | 6.5 |
| 3 | 120# | 5 | 7 |
| 4 | 120# | 5 | 7.5 |
| 5 | 120# | 5 | 8 |

**Notes:** 120# is ~78% of 155# 1RM, the upper end of the volume zone. **Do not** test a single today — press is parked through ~5/18 (`§4`). Brace ribs down, glutes squeezed, drive bar past forehead, lock biceps to ears at top.

## 2. Weighted Chin-up — Vertical Pull (superset with #3)

**Every 2:30 for 4 sets:**
- Weighted chin-up (palms toward you) **3 reps @ 25#**
- Face pull, rope, light cable **15 reps**

**Notes — chin-ups:** Lighter load than Mon's 35# pull-ups, and chin-up grip (more biceps) is a slightly different stimulus. Goal is clean reps, full hang to chin-over-bar. If 25# × 3 feels easy across all 4, next time try 30# × 3.

**Notes — face pulls:** This is the *priority* finisher of the week. You logged your first-ever direct rear-delt sets on 4/27. Keep the cadence — high elbows, pull rope to forehead, pause 1 sec. **Do not skip this section.** Even if you're tired, do these.

## 3. Anti-extension Core — 3 sets, not for time

- Hanging knee raise or strict toes-to-bar **6 reps**
- Side plank **30 sec each side**

**Notes:** Slow controlled negatives on the knee raise. No swing.
