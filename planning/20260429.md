# Wed 4/29 — Strict Press (Volume Build) + Vertical Pull + Hypertrophy Shoulders

> **Header:** Priority lift today is strict press — building the max via volume per `§4` (no 1RM until ~5/18, accumulated 5×5 @ 70-78% is the path). Also closing `§3` #1 (vertical pull) with chin-ups, and `§3` #2 (rear delt under target) with two direct hits — face pulls + lateral raises double as the hypertrophy shoulder block.

**Intensity:** 6.5/10
**Duration:** ~55 min

## Warm-up — 8 min

- 3 min easy row or jump rope.
- Empty bar press 2×8 → 95# × 5 → 110# × 3.
- Band pull-aparts 2×15.

## 1. Strict Press — Strength Volume

**Every 2:30 for 5 sets:** 5 reps @ **120#**

| Set | Load | Reps | RPE target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1–2 | 120# | 5 | 6–7 |
| 3–4 | 120# | 5 | 7–7.5 |
| 5 | 120# | 5 | 8 |

**Notes:** 120# is ~78% of 155# 1RM — top end of the volume zone, this is where the press max will rebuild from. **Do not** test a single. Brace ribs down, glutes squeezed, drive bar past forehead, lock biceps to ears at top. If all 5 sets feel ≤ RPE 7, next press day push to 122-125#.

## 2. DB OHP + Chest-Supported Row — Hypertrophy Push/Pull Superset

**Every 2:00 for 4 sets:**
- DB overhead press **8-10 reps @ 40# each**
- Chest-supported DB row (incline bench, face down) **10 reps @ 50# each**

**Notes — DB OHP:** Different stimulus than the BB press — full ROM, each side stabilizing on its own, hits triceps and side delt harder. Pick a load where 8 is a grinder and 10 is the cap.

**Notes — chest-supported row:** True antagonist to the press; chest on the bench keeps the low back out of it, so this won't tax you for Thu's RDL day. Pull DBs to your hips, hard scap squeeze, controlled lower. Fallback if no incline bench: Pendlay row 10 @ 95#, or seal row, or one-arm DB row (10/side — doubles the section length).

Bumped to 4 sets since you've got capacity — push/pull supersets recover faster than straight sets.

## 3. Weighted Chin-up — Vertical Pull (superset with #4)

**Every 2:30 for 4 sets:**
- Weighted chin-up (palms toward you) **3 reps @ 25#**
- Face pull, rope, light cable **15 reps**

**Notes — chin-ups:** Mon you did 35# pull-ups and missed the last rep — chins at 25# are a deload-feel rebuild. Full hang to chin-over-bar, no kip. If 25# × 3 is clean across all 4, next time bump to 30#.

**Notes — face pulls:** **Do not skip this.** Rear delt is `§3` #2's flagged deficit (only 4 sets this window, target ≥6/wk). High elbows, pull rope to forehead, 1-sec pause. Today + Sat's face pulls clear the deficit for the week.

## 4. Hypertrophy Side Delt + Core — 3 sets

- DB lateral raise **12 reps @ 10-12#** (slow eccentric, no swing)
- Hanging knee raise **8 reps** (controlled, no swing)

**Notes:** Lateral raises are a second hit on the rear-delt-adjacent side delt — keep the load light, the point is mid-range tension at 12 reps, not max weight. Knee raises are anti-extension core (`§3` `core — anti-extension`).
