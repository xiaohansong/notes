## 3. DB Bench — Horizontal Push Hypertrophy

**3 sets:** 10 reps @ **65# DBs**, rest 90 sec

**Notes:** Last horizontal push was 4/26 (barbell bench 185 5×5, missed rep). DB bench gives you a different stimulus — wider ROM, harder lockout — without re-stressing the failed barbell pattern.
