# Thu 4/30 — Posterior Chain + Carries

> **Header:** Addresses `Balance §3` #3 (conditioning modality variety — sandbag/farmer carry, no KB swing) and hinge volume progression (RDL 198→205# track). Quad/hinge ratio currently 1.21:1 — extra hinge volume rebalances toward 1:1.

**Intensity:** 7/10
**Duration:** ~55 min

## Warm-up — 8 min

- 5 min easy row or bike.
- Hip hinge drill with empty bar, 2×8.
- Glute bridges 2×10.
- RDL ramp: 95# × 8, 135# × 5, 175# × 3.

## 1. Romanian Deadlift — Hinge Strength

**Every 2:30 for 5 sets:** 5 reps @ **205#** (tempo 20X1, 2-sec lower)

| Set | Load | Reps | Tempo |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1–5 | 205# | 5 | 2 sec down, control up |

**Notes:** +7# from your 4/23 RDL session (195# 10×5). Lower-rep, heavier — less reps but more bar weight, leaning into the strength end. Bar travels straight down the legs, hinge at hip, soft knees, pull bar tight against you the whole way. Stop at mid-shin or wherever lat control breaks first.

## 2. Loaded Carry — Conditioning + Anti-lateral Core

**5 rounds, not for time, ~90s rest:**
- Sandbag bear hug carry or farmer carry (whichever you have): **40 ft × 2** (down and back) at 70-100# total
- Walk back

**Notes:** This is the conditioning piece. **No KB swings this week** — 4/22 used the slot, max 1/wk per `notes/0420-0425`. Stand tall, ribs stacked, breathe through the nose. Heavy enough that the last 10 ft is hard.

## 3. GHD Back Extension — Anti-flexion

**3 sets:**
- 10 reps, last rep 10-sec hold at top
- Rest 60 sec between sets

**Notes:** Same protocol that worked on 4/21. Squeeze glutes hard at top, don't hyperextend. The 10-sec hold is the point — don't skip it.

## 4. Optional Finisher (skip if tight on time)

- Tricep pushdown 3×12 @ 70#
- Lateral raise 3×10 @ 10#
